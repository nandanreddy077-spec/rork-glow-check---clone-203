## ðŸš¨ Risks & Mitigation

### Risk 1: Users don't see results
**Mitigation**: 
- Set realistic expectations (4-6 weeks for visible changes)
- Show small wins early (hydration improves in days)
- Celebrate non-visual progress (better sleep, less stress)

### Risk 2: Privacy concerns with photos
**Mitigation**:
- All photos stored locally + encrypted
- Clear privacy policy
- Option to blur faces in community sharing
- Never sell personal data

### Risk 3: Too complex for casual users
**Mitigation**:
- Onboarding explains value in <60 seconds
- Quick logging mode (30 seconds)
- Smart defaults to reduce friction
- Progressive disclosure of features
